## Canonical Homepage Pricing Structure

### 1. Free Demo Download

- `$0`
- public signed installer
- install Prompt Forge and test the real app before paying
- the demo is capped, but it gives the actual workstation experience

Includes access to 5 free lanes:

- Photography
- Cinematic
- Watercolor
- Pixel Art
- Graphic Design

Best for:

- trying Prompt Forge
- testing the workflow
- seeing whether structured prompt control fits how the user creates

CTA:

- `Download Free Demo`

### 2. Prompt Forge App Unlock

- `$19.99`
- one-time purchase
- this is the dominant / featured homepage pricing card

Includes:

- removes the demo prompt-copy cap
- activates Prompt Forge on the user's machine through the unlock-file flow
- includes 1 locked premium lane of the user's choice
- includes 1 week complimentary group-session access
- includes limited email help/tips for one specific image project
- feature suggestions from purchasers receive higher-priority consideration

Best for:

- users who want the full app unlocked without committing to longer support access

CTA:

- `How Unlock Works`

### 3. Group Session Access

This is subordinate to App Unlock and should not visually replace it as the main product.

Monthly:

- `$6.99`
- 1 month group-session access
- 1 permanent locked premium lane unlock

Quarterly:

- `$17.99`
- 3 months group-session access
- 5 permanent locked premium lane unlocks

Yearly:

- `$49.99`
- 1 year group-session access
- all locked premium lanes permanently unlocked

Important note:

- lane unlocks are permanent
- group-session access is time-based
- users do not have to attend sessions if they only want the included lane unlocks

CTA:

- `Contact to Purchase`

### 4. Manual Fulfillment

Tone:

- intentional
- trustworthy
- not apologetic

Approved model:

- direct purchase
- manual unlock
- real contact

Accepted payment methods:

- PayPal
- Cash App
- credit/debit card

Commercial/custom lane work:

- separate
- quoted individually

CTA:

- `Email WindySoliloquy@gmail.com`

## Homepage Layout Intent

When this section is rendered on the homepage:

- `Prompt Forge App Unlock` should be the dominant card
- `Free Demo Download` should support it, not overpower it
- group-session plans should appear below as optional support / lane-unlock bundles
- manual fulfillment should read as part of the direct relationship model, not as a broken checkout

## Drift To Avoid

Do not drift the homepage pricing back toward:

- generic `Premium Support` as the only `$49.99` interpretation
- checkout-first language
- vague `support tier` language that hides lane unlocks
- missing free-lane list
- missing group-session permanence note
- language that suggests the website itself automates purchase or unlock delivery
